Verification Guide

How to spot fake news like a fact-checker

🟢 Signals of Legitimate News
Named, verifiable sources
Real experts and officials cited by full name and title — you can look them up independently.
Neutral, factual writing tone
Reports facts without making you angry or scared. Opinion pieces are clearly labeled as such.
Author byline and publication date
A named journalist with a track record and a clear timestamp you can verify.
Multiple outlets reporting the same story
If it's real and important, independent outlets confirm it. One unknown site only = suspicious.
Editorial transparency and corrections
Reputable outlets issue corrections. They have About pages, editorial teams, and contact info.
🔴 Red Flags for Fake News
Sensational or clickbait headlines
ALL CAPS, excessive punctuation!!!, "You won't BELIEVE..." — triggers clicks, not understanding.
Anonymous or unverifiable sources
"Sources say...", "insiders report..." — without names, these could be completely invented.
Out-of-context images or videos
Old photos repurposed as breaking news. Always reverse-image-search suspicious visuals.
Poor grammar and spelling
Professional outlets have editors. Frequent errors signal low quality or rushed fabrication.
Impersonator domain names
"ABCnews.com.co" or "CNN-breaking.net" — check the full URL very carefully before trusting.
✅ 7-Step Verification Checklist
01🔗
Domain check — Is it a known, established outlet?
02🗓️
Date check — Is this current, or recycled old news?
03🖼️
Image check — Reverse-image-search any photos
04📰
Cross-check — Are other outlets reporting this too?
05👤
Author check — Does the journalist have a track record?
06🧩
Body check — Does the article actually support the headline?
07🌐
Fact-checker — Search on Snopes, PolitiFact, or AltNews

About This Project
TruthLens is an AI-powered fake news detection system built with Python, NLP, and supervised ML. It uses TF-IDF vectorization with Logistic Regression and Passive Aggressive Classifier to analyze linguistic patterns and determine credibility — enhanced with real-time Gemini AI analysis for deeper context understanding.
